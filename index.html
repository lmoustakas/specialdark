Special Session on the Astrophysical Constraints of Dark Matter Properties

Thursday, January 7, 2016, 2:00pm - 3:30pm

What is dark matter, and how can astronomical observations give insights or measurements of its detailed particle properties? How do we coordinate work across vast astronomical approaches? How best can astronomers and particle physicists share ideas and advances? Come to this session to learn new ideas on this front, and to contribute to the discussion!
